Define the Problem

Create a mechanical toy for people who enjoy DIY projects.

Design Challenge:

Do-it-yourself home projects are becoming increasingly popular. People are often looking for beginner to intermediate home craft projects. Design and model a mechanical toy that can be assembled with small brads and/or glue.

Constraints:

  • Must be a free standing toy.
  • May not have a footprint that exceeds 6" depth x 12" width x 10" height
  • Must be manually operated.
  • Must have a minimum of three separate motions or moving actions once assembled.
  • Must be made of wood or plastic.
  • Motions should be cause/effect.
